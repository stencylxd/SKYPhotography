How can I use these images?

If you want to use a picture, shoot me an email telling me which one you want.
You can pay me using a service called PayPal. Upon buying the image, you will
receive a paid-for, unwatermarked version of the image. You may use it according
to the licenses shown below.

Unpaid for images are licensed under the Creative Commons license CC-BY-NC-ND 4.0.
Paid-for images are licensed under the Creative Commons license CC-BY-SA 4.0.
Please don't steal / share paid-for images.
