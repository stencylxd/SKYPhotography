How can I use these images?

If you want to use a picture, shoot me an email telling me which one you want.
You can pay me using a service called PayPal. Upon buying the image, you will
recieve a paid-for, unwatermarked version of the image. You may use it according
to the licences shown at the bottom.

Sizes:

Sizes are measured in inches, and prices are in USD.
4x6 = $5
5x7 = $10
8x10 = $20
11x14 = $28
16x20 = $40
