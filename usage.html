How can I use these images?

If you want a picture, shoot me an email telling me which one you want.
You can pay me using a service called PayPal. Upon buying, you will
receive a unwatermarked hangable version of the photo in the mail.
You can choose the size. If you want to use digital version, all gallery
images are licensed under CC-BY-NC-ND.
